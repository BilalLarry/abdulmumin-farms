About Our Farm

ABDUL MUMIN ISSAH FARMS was established in 2010 with a vision to provide high-quality livestock while maintaining sustainable farming practices. Our farm spans over 50 acres of lush green land, providing the perfect environment for our animals to thrive.

We take pride in our ethical farming methods, ensuring all our animals are raised with care, proper nutrition, and in hygienic conditions. Our commitment to quality has made us a trusted name in the farming community.
